Technology (UF vs RO)

Aquara uses ultrafiltration (UF) for practical outdoor hydration—high usability without pumps or wastewater.

UF (Ultrafiltration)

UF membranes can help reduce microbes (bacteria/parasites) and suspended solids. They are commonly used in portable filtration because they operate without high pressure or electricity.

Why not RO in bottles?

Reverse osmosis typically needs high pressure, produces wastewater, and has low flow in handheld form factors. That’s why most portable bottles use UF/MF rather than RO.
